How vulnerable are they to typhoons?(click to reveal answer)

As it is known, the Philippines is situated in an area along the “Pacific Typhoon Belt”. This area of the planet is especially vulnerable to calamities like Typhoons and Tropical Cyclones. Seeing as this zone contains favorable atmospheric conditions which is a factor necessary for typhoon formation. Moreover, not only does our country’s geographical location contribute to the community’s vulnerability, but so does the underdevelopment of our country. The poverty of our nation provides us with poor infrastructure or housing conditions, and gives us limited access to the resources we need to be prepared in the case of calamities like typhoons. Additionally, the concentration of population in coastal areas makes the country more vulnerable and prone to dangers linked with typhoons, namely floods and surges.
